Organizing Stringsclick to collapse
When cataloguing a collection of genetic strings, we should have an established system by which to organize them. 
The standard method is to organize strings as they would appear in a dictionary, so that "APPLE" precedes "APRON", 
which in turn comes before "ARMOR".
